# 🌿 AgroLens AI

AgroLens AI is an AI-powered plant disease detection and advisory platform designed to help farmers identify crop diseases quickly using leaf images. The system analyzes uploaded plant leaf images and provides disease diagnosis, treatment recommendations, and preventive measures in an easy-to-understand format.

The platform also includes multilingual voice assistance, plant health scoring, weather-based insights, and downloadable reports to make the solution practical for real-world agricultural use.

## 📌 Problem Statement

Farmers often struggle to identify plant diseases at an early stage due to lack of expert guidance and limited access to agricultural resources. Incorrect diagnosis or delayed treatment can result in crop damage, reduced yield, and financial losses.

AgroLens AI aims to bridge this gap by providing a simple AI-based solution that enables farmers to diagnose crop diseases instantly using a smartphone.

---

## 💡 Solution

AgroLens AI allows users to upload or capture a leaf image of a crop. The system analyzes the image using an AI-based disease detection model and provides:

- Disease identification
- Plant health score
- Possible causes of the disease
- Treatment recommendations
- Preventive measures
- Links to recommended agricultural medicines
- Weather conditions affecting crop health

The platform also supports multilingual interaction to ensure accessibility for farmers in different regions.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🌱 AI Plant Disease Detection
Users can upload a plant leaf image and receive instant disease predictions.

### 📊 Plant Health Score
A health score is generated to indicate the severity of the detected disease.

### ☁️ Weather Insights
Live weather conditions are displayed to highlight environmental factors that influence disease occurrence.

### 🗣️ Multilingual Voice Assistant
Farmers can listen to treatment advice and interact with the system in their native language.

### 💊 Treatment Recommendations
The platform suggests practical treatment plans and provides links to recommended agricultural medicines.

### 📄 PDF Report Generation
Users can download a detailed report containing diagnosis results, treatment advice, and precautions.

### 📚 Plant Guide
The system includes a guide covering multiple crops and common plant diseases.

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. The farmer uploads or captures a leaf image.
2. The system processes the image using an AI disease detection model.
3. The platform identifies the disease and calculates a plant health score.
4. The system generates detailed information about the disease, including causes and treatment options.
5. Weather conditions are displayed to explain possible environmental triggers.
6. The farmer can listen to advice in their selected language or download a detailed report.
